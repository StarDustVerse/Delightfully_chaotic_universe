Supernova Dust Formation as a Function of Progenitor Mass

Institution: Indian Institute of Astrophysics, Bangalore

Supervisor: Dr. Arkaprabha Sarangi

Analyzed dust formation in CCSNe using progenitor models (8–120 M⊙). Quantified the yields of carbon, silicate, alumina, and CO using statistical techniques and visual analysis.

Star Clusters as Gamma-ray Emitters

Institution: Laboratoire APC – Université Paris Cité

Supervisors: Dr. Stefano Gabici, Dr. Giada Peron, Dr. Marcella Brusa

Explored gamma-ray emissions from young star clusters using Fermi-LAT data. Performed cross-matching, Monte Carlo simulations, and spatial/spectral analysis.

Acoustic Levitation for Microgravity Simulation

Institution: University of Kerala

Supervisor: Dr. Bijini B. R.

Built an Arduino-based acoustic levitation setup to simulate microgravity for droplets. Studied how physical variables influence levitation stability.

Multiwavelength Astrophysics Projects

  • Radio Imaging of Abell 3670: Calibrated and imaged JVLA data with CASA to study extended radio sources.
  • Faraday Rotation Mapping: Created RM maps to analyze magnetic fields in the Coma Cluster.
  • X-ray AGN Classification: Used XSPEC to model AGN spectra and extract physical parameters.
  • Galaxy Cluster Gas Modeling: Simulated hydrostatic equilibrium and metal diffusion in the ICM.
  • SNR Shock Evolution: Simulated 1D shock propagation and modeled X-ray emission from SNRs.
  • Photometry of NGC 2808: Performed PSF photometry on SOFI J/K-band images using IRAF.
  • Spectroscopic Classification: Reduced stellar spectra and identified spectral types from line profiles.
